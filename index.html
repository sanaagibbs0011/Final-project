How to stay positive:

People struggle with trying to stay optimistic and thinking positive things. The best way to remain staying positive is to mediate that's the best option. Mediating is the best way to calm you and you're mind. Usually when people mediate, they either listen to calm soothing music to help calm they're mind. down. Another way to stay positive is to say positive affrimations.For example, "I deserve happiness and joy." Some people make up their own affrimations, and say it in the mirror as much as they like.

Write yourself a love letter

On a blank sheet of paper, write down positive things that you like about yourself for example goals that you achieve and qualities that you love about yourself. After you've done that, read your letter three times a day and you will continue to love yourself. And try to remeber positive things that you like yourself.
